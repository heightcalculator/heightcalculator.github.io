Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

Height Calculator ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our height calculation service at heightcalculator.github.io.

Information We Collect

We believe in maximum privacy. Our calculator performs all calculations in your browser, and we do not collect, store, or transmit your height data or calculation results. We may collect:

  • Basic analytics data (page views, device type, browser type)
  • Information you voluntarily provide through contact forms
  • Cookies for essential website functionality

How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our website functionality and user experience
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our service
  • Ensure the security and integrity of our website

Data Security

Since all calculations happen directly in your browser, your height data never leaves your device. We do not store any calculation data on our servers. Any information you provide through contact forms is transmitted securely and stored with industry-standard security measures.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and website hosting. These services may collect information about your visit, but they are bound by their own privacy policies. We ensure that any third-party services we use comply with data protection regulations.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure proper website functionality. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of any communications from us

Children's Privacy

Our service is available to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at waqas.pccp@gmail.com.
